Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Here is how VidoGrab handles information.

1. Overview

VidoGrab (“we,” “us,” or “our”) operates the website vidograb.com, a free online video downloader. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide

When you use VidoGrab, you may paste a video URL into our form. We process this URL temporarily to fetch download links. We do not require account registration and do not ask for your name, address, or payment information.

Automatically collected information

Our servers may automatically collect limited technical data, including:

  • IP address (used for rate limiting and abuse prevention)
  • Browser type and device information
  • Date and time of requests
  • Referring page URL

3. How We Use Information

We use collected information solely to:

  • Process your video download requests
  • Protect the service from abuse and maintain rate limits
  • Improve reliability, performance, and security
  • Respond to support or legal inquiries when you contact us

4. Data Storage & Retention

VidoGrab does not permanently store downloaded videos or the URLs you submit. Video URLs are processed in real time and discarded after your session. Server logs containing IP addresses may be retained for a limited period for security and troubleshooting, then automatically deleted.

5. Cookies & Third-Party Services

VidoGrab may use essential cookies or similar technologies required for basic site functionality. We may also integrate third-party services such as Cloudflare Turnstile for bot protection. These services may set their own cookies subject to their respective privacy policies.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not use your data for targeted advertising through VidoGrab itself.

6. Third-Party Links & Platforms

When you submit a URL, our service communicates with third-party platforms and APIs to retrieve publicly available video metadata and download links. Your use of those platforms is governed by their own privacy policies. VidoGrab is not responsible for the data practices of third-party websites or services.

7. Children’s Privacy

VidoGrab is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data we hold about you. Since we collect minimal data and do not maintain user accounts, most requests can be addressed by contacting us directly.

9. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information processed through VidoGrab. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Your continued use of VidoGrab after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at vidograb.ads@gmail.com or visit our Contact Us page.
